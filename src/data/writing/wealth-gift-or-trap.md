# Is wealth a gift of God or a trap?

I've long heard that wealth was a symbol of God's blessing in the old testament  (think Abraham, Joseph, Boaz, David, Solomon, etc). Is that true? Even if it was then, is being wealthy today a gift that God gives bless people or a temptation and test that he [allows](https://www.bible.com/bible/59/JAS.1.13.ESV) them to endure?

There's no shortage of quotes from Jesus warning his followers about the dangers of riches, and Jesus himself was not materially wealthy.
- Matt 6:19-21
- Matt 19:23-24
- Mark 10:23-25
- Matt 6:24
- Luke 6:24
- Mark 4:19

While that certainly doesn't make it a sin, nor do we have passages where Jesus condemns the rich for being rich, rather he condems them for [...].
Still, there are *many* more references in the bible that warn against riches than that exalt it's merits and advantages.
